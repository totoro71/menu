You might not be aware, but Crown Candy Kitchen is one of St Louis’ oldest and most popular attractions! Please take a moment to look around. It may feel as if you’ve taken a giant step back in time. Maybe you’ve spotted the vintage jukebox, the coca-cola collectibles or the soda fountain.
